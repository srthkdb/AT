Oraculi

Oraculi is a discussion forum designed for IITK students to help them clear their doubts.

Features
  • Add Subjects(requires admin's permission)
  • Share files using file sharing centre.
  • Start a discussion thread in a subject.
  • Add comments to answer questions.
  • Add pictures to your questions and comments(optional)
  • View your discussions in My Discussions tab.
  • To download files, right click on the link(+ symbol) select save link as. Do not left click on the + symbol to download files.
